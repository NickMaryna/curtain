about us

Bespoke Curtains & Textile Interiors — Designed with Care, Made for Comfort

Canvas Textile is a team of professionals with over 14 years’ experience in crafting made-to-measure curtains and bespoke soft furnishings.
 We create tailored solutions for both residential and commercial interiors — from individual window treatments to complete textile schemes for entire properties.
We work closely with private homeowners, interior designers, architects, restaurants, and property developers. Whether it’s a single room or a whole home, every detail is thoughtfully designed to suit your personal taste and lifestyle. Our work is about more than just fabric. It’s about creating atmosphere, comfort, and a true sense of home.
Every project starts with your vision and ends with a result that feels unmistakably yours.
We currently serve: • Winchester and surrounding areas • Key towns across Hampshire: Southampton, Basingstoke, Andover • London and Greater London
 If you’re looking for bespoke curtains, made-to-measure window treatments or a complete textile interior service — we’d be delighted to help.
